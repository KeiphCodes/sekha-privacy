Privacy Policy  ·  Effective Date: June 1, 2026  ·  Developed by Ivy Industries

The short version

Sekha does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device. Always.

1. Data We Collect

We collect nothing. Sekha stores all user data — session history, journal entries, voice recordings, streak information, personal statistics, display name, settings, and preferences — exclusively on your device using local device storage.

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server, database, or third party operated by us or anyone else.

2. Analytics and Tracking

Sekha contains zero analytics code, zero tracking scripts, zero advertising SDKs, and zero telemetry of any kind. We do not know how many people use the app, how long sessions last, which features are used, or anything else about your usage patterns.

Your practice is private. We designed it that way intentionally.

3. Third-Party Services

Sekha does not integrate with or transmit data to any third-party services. All features operate entirely on your device with no external network connections required to use the app.

4. Voice and Audio Recordings

The journal feature allows you to make optional voice recordings to accompany session notes. These recordings are stored locally on your device only. They are never transmitted anywhere. Deleting the app permanently removes all recordings.

Recordings are only saved when you explicitly tap the Save button after a session. If you exit without saving, the temporary recording is discarded immediately.

5. Data Backup

Sekha offers a manual backup feature that exports your data as a JSON file saved to your device. This file is created by you, saved by you, and controlled entirely by you. We never see, access, or store your backup files.

6. Children's Privacy

Sekha does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no data from anyone, this applies universally.

7. Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and nothing is transmitted to external servers, the security of your data is determined by your device's own security measures. We recommend keeping your device updated and using a device lock screen.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy in a way that involves collecting user data, we will notify users prominently within the app before any change takes effect and update the effective date above. We do not anticipate this ever changing — the no-collection principle is fundamental to how Sekha is built.
